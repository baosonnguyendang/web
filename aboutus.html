Who we are

Established in 2020, HASH is a global leader in digital interactive entertainment. HASH develops and delivers games, content and online services for Internet-connected consoles, mobile devices and personal computers. HASH has more than 1 million registered players around the world.

In the first half of 2020, HASH had an revenue of over $1.5 million. Headquartered in Di An, Binh Duong, HASH is recognized for a portfolio of critically acclaimed, high-quality brands.
